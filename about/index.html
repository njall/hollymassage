--- layout: default title: About --- Practitioner: Caoilfhionn Warne - Diploma in Sports Massage Caoilfhionn 'Holly' Warne is an international athlete with experience of a range of pressures athletes face. She represented Ireland in the 2011 Roller Derby World Cup in Toronto, and was selected for the training squad in 2016-2017. Alongside sports, Holly is an accomplished musician, and has held a variety of roles from teaching through to office work. She taught sports, anatomy and physiology for the West Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service as part of an Active Leisure BTEC. Holly is aware of the physical strain which can accompany any working environment, whether active or sedentary, and can offer a tailored massage therapy to each sportsperson to meet their needs.
